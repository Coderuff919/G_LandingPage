Discover a transformative hideaway where warm hospitality meets the authentic charm and scenic beauty of Ubud, Bali. Nestled among 350 acres of rolling rice fields and oak tree groves, Heritage Ubud features an 10,500-square-foot spa, a zero-edge resort pool, three signature dining options, including a seasonal poolside restaurant, inspired by the region’s culinary heritage and seamless access to Ubud, Bali’s renowned artisan shops and outdoor experiences.

With its captivating blend of artistic country charm, natural beauty and impeccable arts, Ubud, Bali consistently ranks as one of Indonesia’s favorite small towns. Whether scrolling your way through artisan shops or unwinding amid understated luxury, this historic enclave is certain to suit your feeling. Located in close proximity to the region’s renowned arts and access to exclusive shopping opportunities, Heritage Ubud invites you on a journey into the heart and soul of Bali, Indonesia.
